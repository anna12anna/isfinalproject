What are the three best StarWars Movies?
  1. Episode III
    • Epic Obi-Wan and Anakin fight scene - a visually stunning and emotionally charged culmination.
    • For democracy - showcases pivotal moments that shape the galactic political landscape.
  2. Episode IV
    • Classic stuff - introduces the iconic world of Star Wars and sets the foundation for the saga.
    • Harrison Ford - debuts the charismatic and beloved character, Han Solo.
  3. Episode II
    • So cheesy - memorable for its unique blend of classic Star Wars charm and melodrama.
    • Funny - offers light-hearted moments and humorous dialogues amidst intense scenes.
